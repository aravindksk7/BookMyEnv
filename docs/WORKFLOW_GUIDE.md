## FAQ

### Booking Questions

**Q: Can I extend my booking after it starts?**
A: Yes, edit the booking and adjust the end date. Check for conflicts with any new refreshes that may have been scheduled.

**Q: What happens if a refresh starts during my booking?**
A: You'll receive notifications before the refresh. Your test data may be lost depending on the impact type. Contact the refresh owner to coordinate.

**Q: How do I prevent my booking from being disrupted?**
A: Mark it as "Critical" and set priority to "Critical" or "High". This increases conflict severity, requiring force approval for any overlapping refresh.

### Refresh Questions

**Q: How far in advance should I plan a refresh?**
A: At least 7 days for standard refreshes, 14+ days for major refreshes affecting multiple environments.

**Q: What if my refresh is rejected?**
A: Review the rejection reason, adjust timing using "Suggest Alternative Slots", and resubmit. Contact the approver for clarification if needed.

**Q: Can I cancel a refresh after it's approved?**
A: Yes, you can cancel before execution starts. Navigate to the intent and click Cancel.

### Conflict Questions

**Q: Who decides the conflict severity?**
A: The system calculates it automatically based on booking priority, impact type, and test phase.

**Q: What does "Force Approval" mean?**
A: It overrides MAJOR conflicts, allowing the refresh to proceed despite critical bookings. Requires Admin/EnvManager role and documented justification.

**Q: How do I see all my conflicts?**
A: Navigate to Refresh â†’ Conflicts for refresh conflicts, or Bookings â†’ Conflicts tab for booking conflicts.
